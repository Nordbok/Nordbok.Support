📘 Nordbok – Privacy Policy & Data Collection

Complete overview of data types, usage, link to identity, and tracking

Overview

This document provides a full overview of the types of data collected in the Nordbok app, how they are used, whether they are linked to the user’s identity, and whether they are used for tracking. This reflects the selections made for App Store and Google Play.

📂 Contact Information

  • Name – Linked to identity: Yes | Tracking: No
  • Email address – Linked to identity: Yes | Tracking: Yes
  • Phone number – Linked to identity: Yes | Tracking: Yes

💳 Financial Information

  • Payment information – Linked to identity: Yes | Tracking: No

📇 Contacts

  • Contacts – Linked to identity: Yes | Tracking: No

✉️ User-Generated Content

  • Emails or text messages – Linked to identity: Yes | Tracking: No
  • Photos or videos – Linked to identity: Yes | Tracking: Yes

🔎 User Activity

  • Search history – Linked to identity: Yes | Tracking: Yes
  • Purchase history – Linked to identity: Yes | Tracking: Yes
  • Product interaction – Linked to identity: Yes | Tracking: Yes
  • Other usage data – Linked to identity: Yes | Tracking: Yes

🔧 Diagnostics & Performance

  • Crash data – Linked to identity: Yes | Tracking: No
  • Performance data – Linked to identity: Yes | Tracking: Yes

🆔 Identifiers

  • User ID – Linked to identity: Yes | Tracking: Yes
  • Device ID – Linked to identity: Yes | Tracking: Yes

📁 Other Data

  • Other data types – Linked to identity: Yes | Tracking: Yes

✅ Summary

Nordbok collects only the data necessary to provide app functionality, analytics, personalization, and marketing. Certain data types are used for tracking via third-party SDKs, in line with App Store and Google Play guidelines.
