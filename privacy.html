Overview

This document provides a full overview of the types of data collected in the Nordbok app, how they are used, whether they are linked to the user’s identity, and whether they are used for tracking. This reflects the selections made for the App Store.

📂 Contact Information

  • Name — Linked to identity: Yes | Tracking: No
  • Email address — Linked to identity: Yes | Tracking: Yes
  • Phone number — Linked to identity: Yes | Tracking: Yes

💳 Financial Information

  • Payment information — Linked to identity: Yes | Tracking: No

📇 Contacts

  • Contacts — Linked to identity: Yes | Tracking: No

✉️ User-Generated Content

  • Emails or text messages — Linked to identity: Yes | Tracking: No
  • Photos or videos — Linked to identity: Yes | Tracking: Yes

🔎 User Activity

  • Search history — Linked to identity: Yes | Tracking: Yes
  • Purchase history — Linked to identity: Yes | Tracking: Yes
  • Product interaction — Linked to identity: Yes | Tracking: Yes
  • Other usage data — Linked to identity: Yes | Tracking: Yes

🔧 Diagnostics & Performance

  • Crash data — Linked to identity: Yes | Tracking: No
  • Performance data — Linked to identity: Yes | Tracking: Yes

🆔 Identifiers

  • User ID — Linked to identity: Yes | Tracking: Yes
  • Device ID — Linked to identity: Yes | Tracking: Yes

📁 Other Data

  • Other data types — Linked to identity: Yes | Tracking: Yes

App Tracking Transparency (ATT)

If the app engages in tracking as defined by Apple, Nordbok will request your permission via the AppTrackingTransparency prompt. You can change your choice at any time in iOS Settings.

Vilkår for bruk

Ved å bruke Nordbok godtar du følgende vilkår.

1. Generelt

Nordbok er for personlig bruk. Innholdet er beskyttet av opphavsrett og må ikke kopieres uten tillatelse.

2. Abonnement

Auto-fornyende abonnement. Betaling via Apple-konto ved kjøp. Fornyes automatisk med mindre kansellert minst 24 timer før fornyelse i App Store-innstillinger.

3. Brukeransvar

Du er ansvarlig for aktivitet via din Apple-ID i appen. Misbruk kan føre til utestenging.

4. Endringer

Vilkårene kan oppdateres. Nye versjoner publiseres her; videre bruk regnes som aksept.

5. Kontakt
